Pripyat map and POI

  • Low: Levels near or equal to normal background radiation. You can stay in these spots for long periods of time, including weeks.
  • Medium: Levels slightly higher than background radiation. You can stay there for days without danger.
  • High: Levels considerably higher than background radiation. You can stay there for hours. However, it is recommended to reduce exposure time as much as possible.
  • Extreme: Levels in some spots can reach lethal doses within hours. It is recommended to minimize exposure (do not exceed one hour) and wear masks due to alpha and beta particles suspended in the air (FFP2 minimum, FFP3 optimal).
