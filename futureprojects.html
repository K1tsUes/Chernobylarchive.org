FUTURE PROJECTS // CHERNOBYLARCHIVE.ORG

Our organization is actively developing three next-generation initiatives dedicated to the preservation, mapping, and virtual exploration of the Chernobyl Exclusion Zone. Discover our digital and ecological pipeline below.

INITIATIVE // 01

#SavetheZone

A comprehensive environmental and historical preservation campaign designed to achieve an ecologically sustainable future for the Chernobyl Exclusion Zone. Through international petitions, targeted awareness campaigns, and strategic alliances with leading universities, we mobilize networks of academic researchers and dedicated volunteers to protect this unique habitat from degradation and preserve its historic memory.

STATUS: ACTIVE CAMPAIGN
SYSTEM // 02

RadiaMap

An advanced geospatial monitoring infrastructure combining an integrated web module and a dedicated mobile application. RadiaMap utilizes autonomous robotic rovers and static IoT telemetry sensors deployed across the exclusion zone to feed a live, real-time radiation map. This data pipeline allows researchers and the public to safely track radiation fluctuations across highly contaminated areas.

STATUS: IN DEVELOPMENT // ALPHA PHASE
SIMULATION // 03

U4 Virtual Tour

A fully immersive digital environment offering a precise virtual tour through the high-risk interior of Chernobyl's Unit 4. Developed using autonomous mapping bots, the platform integrates detailed web-based educational minigames. Looking ahead, our technical milestone is the deployment of a high-fidelity, interactive simulator to study RBMK control room dynamics in real time.

STATUS: PROTOYPING
